    *This can help as a starting point for creating many plans or options to structure routines and time. Having a few plans to draw from or multiple routines to draw from can help with sustaining employement. It allows for some flexibility while also being able to maintain a routine. For example having 2 or 3 morning routines. 1 main and 2 backups because life is rarely ever perfect all the time. So if breakfast is never to be skipped, finding a breakfast that can sit in the freezer for the times when breakfast cannot be made would be a good strategy. Having a few bus or transit options available or known is ideal too. This can allow for variations in transit options and transit timing. It requires some work but has a meaningful impact on sustaining employment long term because there isnt only one option eing relied on.*
